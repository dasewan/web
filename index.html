What are the application requirements?

Age Requirement

18-55 years old. The borrower must be between 18 and 55 years old. According to regulatory requirements in the credit industry, our products do not offer loans to current university students. If you are a current university student, please refrain from applying.

ID Card And Bank Card Requirement

1: You need your own valid ID card. When uploading a photo of your ID, ensure it is clear and complete.

2: Only personal bank cards are supported. It is recommended to use bank cards from major national banks; regional or local cards may have restrictions.

Other Information Requirements

1: Contact information. You must provide emergency contact information of a genuine relationship. Falsifying emergency contacts will result in rejection of your application.

2: Employment information. You must provide genuine employment information. Falsifying employment information will result in rejection of your application.

Authorization

During the authentication process, the app will request access to your phone's location, contacts, and SMS permissions. Please agree to these authorizations. If you do not agree, your application may not be completed.
